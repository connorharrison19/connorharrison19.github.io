About

Full-stack content and conversation design

I'm a content and AI conversation design leader with 8+ years across the full product lifecycle. My focus is the orchestration era, where content becomes the connective tissue between channels, ensuring a fluid experience as users move between automated and human-led interactions. I'm drawn to the gaps between service design, traditional UI, and complex technical logic, and I design to close them.

My path here spans hospitality, journalism, and marketing, disciplines that shaped how I think about narrative, empathy, and systems. Beyond client work, I'm a practice builder: I grow design teams through internal playbooks, hiring, and partnering with commercial leads to translate design thinking into new business.

TELUS Expert Messaging

Architected the cross-channel content strategy for the first asynchronous messaging app in Canadian Telecom, a product that lets customers describe their issue and go about their day while a human expert resolves it. My role spanned the entire stack: from IVR prompts and bot triage flows to agent training, marketing collateral, and future-state design.

What I solved

IVR drop-off → Replaced value-prop language with keyword-driven brevity, improving containment rate
Bot-to-expert handoff → Simplified transition language to reduce cognitive load at the handoff moment
Agent consistency → Built AWS content snippets and a printable rubric to standardize first message resolution
Content ecosystem → Served as primary DRI across hundreds of bilingual copy artifacts for dev handoff
$2.5M Projected annual call-offload savings
400K+ Conversations handled through messaging
6 min Reduction in average handling time vs. voice
+9 pts Lift in First Contact Resolution vs. voice
24/7 Support, with 30% of conversations outside business hours
5 pp Direct FCR increase from content strategy
